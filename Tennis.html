Table tennis

Table tennis is considered the fastest ball sport and is therefore ideally suited to train concentration, speed and body control and makes fun on every level.

Whether as a recreational player or as a competitive athlete, the courses on the central campus are suitable for everyone. Previous knowledge is not required. However, the course instructors are happy to show the first technical steps for a leisure-oriented table tennis game.

So - get to the green records and play together with the other participants and have fun.

You can bring your own racquets at all times, but there are also simple clubs to rent in the classroom.

We offer an opportunity to play tennis.

From beginners to champions - everyone can find a course that meets his / her requirements.

You can use your own table tennis racket.
